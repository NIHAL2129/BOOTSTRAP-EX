PLACEMENT MODULE

The placement module, developed using Node.js, Express.js, and MongoDB, streamlines the management of placement drives by providing real-time updates. It allows administrators to post new drive information, while students can easily access and track upcoming opportunities. also ensuring that all relevant details are available at a glance. Enhancing communication and coordination between students and placement coordinators.

PLACEMENT FACILITIES

Our college conducts various placement activities, including Aptitude tests, Programming challenges, and MCQ test to prepare students for recruitment processes. These activities enhance problem-solving skills, technical knowledge, and overall readiness. Ensuring students are well-equipped to excel in campus placement drives and secure their desired job opportunities.
